Motivation

  • Current VLA models are learned by one-stage imitation learning.
  • Limiting generalization, specific only to target robot.
  • Lack of reasoning, predicted actions are not interpretable. (unsafe)

Solution?

Two Stage Planner
  • Leverage interpretable and general Navigation Foundation Model for high level planner.
  • Additional low level planner for specific agents.

Navigation Foundation Model (High Level Planner)

  • Collect a massive amount of ego-centric driving videos with language instructions.
  • Label the collected videos with language actions (5 steering angles * 2 throttle levels) in an unsupervised manner.
    TODO: Use SOTA LLMs to label the evidence and reasoning behind the actions.
  • Train VLMs (MiniCPM-v 2.6).
    TODO: Implement CoT-like training (requires larger VLMs).

How do we label language actions?

Pretraining Latent Actions
  • Learning the latent actions between image frames.
  • Using VQ-VAE based objective.
  • We can classify the latent actions with small size of labeled data.
  • Works well!

Diffusion Policy (Low Level Planner)

  • Train diffusion policies to predict specific agent actions (future waypoints).
  • Conditions are high level actions and previous waypoints.
